## Add of basic layout.

````HTML:Basic layout
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
    <head>
        <meta charset="UTF-8">
        <title>sample</title>
    </head>
<body>
    <header class="header">
    </header>
    <div class="wrapper">
        <main class="main">
        </main>
        <div class="sidemenu">
        </div>
    </div>
    <footer class="footer">
    </footer>
</body>
</html>
````
